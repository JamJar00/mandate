# AI Instructions

## Title & Summary
One-line summary of the agent's intent and a short description.

## Purpose
Why this agent exists and the success criteria for correct behavior.

## Scope & Boundaries
Which repositories, directories, files, environments, or systems the agent may act on — and what is explicitly out-of-scope.

## Allowed Actions
A precise, enumerated allow-list of actions the agent is permitted to perform. Prefer short bullet points and concrete examples.

## Prohibited Actions
Explicitly list actions the agent must never perform and any data or systems that are off-limits.

## Data Handling & Privacy
What data the agent can access, how it must be processed (masking, retention), storage and sharing rules, and privacy considerations.

## Human-in-loop & Approvals
When humans must be involved, approval workflows, who can approve, and escalation paths.

## Observability & Audit
Logging, metrics, audit trails, and where to find evidence of agent activity for post-hoc review.

## Safety & Failure Modes
Known risks, safe-fail behaviour, rollback procedures, and how incidents are handled.

## Security & Access
Credentialing, least-privilege rules, secret management, and required access reviews.

## Testing & CI
How the agent is tested, pre-deployment checks (canary, staging), and CI gates that must pass.

## Contact & Governance
Who owns the agent, maintainers, reviewers, and the process for proposing changes.
